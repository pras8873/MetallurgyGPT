SMS Concast 1 5 September 2025 Introduction Material Mechanical Soft Reduction Confidential © SMS group GmbH All rights reserved 2 5 September 2025 MSR & MHR CONCEPT • MSR WINDOW – Mechanical Soft reduction consists on the application of a small controlled reduction to strand in the zone where the liquid cone at the final stage of solidification closes up. The reduction is applied in a progressive series of steps by mean of PR modules with small reduction carried out at each roll • MSR REDUCTION SEQUENCE - The deformation by MSR must be sufficient to counteract porosity and segregation, while at the same time avoid internal cracks in the bloom. The best results have been achieved using a variable reduction taper which increases progressively with the fs value • MHR (Mechanical Hard Reduction) - The centre porosity and the variability of C/Co in the bloom axis can be reduced by implementing a reduction after complete solidification. This technique can be called Mechanical Hard Reduction (MHR).
