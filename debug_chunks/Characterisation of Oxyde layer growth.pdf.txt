Reheat furnaces are usua1ly c1assified according to the way the charge is handled in the furnace and include pusher, rotary-hearth, roller-hearth and walking-beam types. Reheat fumaces are direct-fired by buming a specific fuel with air. The fuels normally used for firing depend on their availability and can be natura! gas, coke oven gas. a mixture of the IWO, or fuel oil.
