SUMMARY OF MSR RECIPE SUBMITTED TO CUSTOMER .................................... 31 2 1. Summary Shigang claimed that the MSR function could not be used from the beginning on the curved CCM3. The intervention was organized to check the issue and to fine-tune the MSR function. After checking at site, together with remote diagnosing by Mr. Mauro Clochiatti, various bugs were found in the PLC program, which mainly related to:  Rolls gap calibration (couldn’t active, or no calibration COMPLETE even though force and gap were stable);  MSR activation (it went wrong in case some set-points were changed during activation process);  Optimization of load sharing logic (to have the speeds of the downstream rolls equal or

--- CHUNK 1 ---
faster than the last MSR roll); The MSR module elastic deformation factor were checked and obtained by pressing the roll on the dummy bar with different forces (up to 900kN). It’s also found that some MSR modules were not in good condition and with mechanical issues like loose bolts and / or deformed cylinders. When site conditions were allowed, MSR metallurgical tests were made mainly on the spring steel and bearing steel. With application of MSR:  Center soundness and V type segregation were clearly improved for spring steel;  Variation of center carbon segregation was much smaller with proper MSR on spring steel;  Center soundness of bearing steel was also improved, but V type segregation was still clearly visible;  Effect of reducing variation of center carbon segregation with proper MSR was also found on bearing steel;  On both grades, carbon analysis showed also that higher carbon content (positive segregation) was found at the position of around 1/4 - 1/5 of thickness to the surface, regardless of MSR or not.
